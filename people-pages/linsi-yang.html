Linsi Yang

Artist & Curator (PR & marketing - design / publication / research)

Linsi Yang is a multidisciplinary artist, curator, and researcher working across video, photography, sculpture, and performance. Shaped by study and life in multiple places, he treats “distance” as both an everyday condition and a working method. His work often takes the form of installations, image-based objects, and time-based pieces that set up simple spatial situations to make the tension between here and elsewhere perceptible, negotiable, and measurable—rearticulating it across emotional, political, historical, and material dimensions.

He is currently pursuing his MFA at SAIC.

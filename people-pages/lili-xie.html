Lili Xie

Artist

Lili Xie engages with the realms of photography, video, performance, and ceramics in her artistic practice. The evolution of her work is an ongoing journey of self-exploration, inquiry, and the deconstruction of her own identity. Through these diverse mediums, she navigates the complex landscape of personal introspection and transformation, using her body as a conduit to express and explore the depths of her inner self.

Her work has been exhibited in England, US, Italy and China.

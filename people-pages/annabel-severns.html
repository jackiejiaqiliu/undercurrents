Annabel Severns

Curator (logistics / exhibition design)

Annabel Severns is a Dual MA Candidate in Modern and Contemporary Art History and Arts Administration and Policy at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago. As an arts administrator, she specializes in curation and logistics management for art fairs and gallery exhibitions. Annabel currently serves as a gallery manager and consultant at Boreas Fine Art, a contemporary art gallery in Chicago, and is also a licensed real estate broker in Florida. Her current research explores the intersections of contemporary gallery design and commercial real estate practices in Chicago’s West Town.
